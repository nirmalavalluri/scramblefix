Word Sorter

Sort any list of words alphabetically, by length, or in reverse — instantly.

How to Use the Word Sorter

Paste any list of words into the box above, choose your sort mode, and click Sort Words. Results appear instantly and can be copied with one click.

  • A → Z — Standard alphabetical order
  • Z → A — Reverse alphabetical order
  • Shortest first — Great for finding short Scrabble plays
  • Longest first — Spot your highest-value words instantly
  • Reverse order — Flip the order of your original list
