Three Ways to Shuffle Your Text

  • Shuffle Words — Randomly reorders every word in your text. Great for creative writing prompts.
  • Shuffle Sentences — Reorders full sentences while keeping each sentence intact. Useful for randomizing quiz answers.
  • Shuffle Letters — Scrambles letters within words (spaces stay in place). Perfect for creating word puzzles.

All shuffling happens in your browser — nothing is sent to a server.
