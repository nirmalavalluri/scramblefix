Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Welcome to ScrambleFix ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit scramblefix.io.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use ScrambleFix. However, we may collect the following automatically:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, and device type via Google Analytics.
  • Letters entered: The scrambled letters you type into the tool are sent to our AI service to generate word results. We do not store or log these inputs.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes (see Section 4).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and improve the ScrambleFix word unscrambler tool
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Ensure the security and performance of our website

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use cookies for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics uses cookies to help us understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Advertising: Google AdSense uses cookies to show relevant ads.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API – Powers our word unscrambling AI. Letters you enter are processed by this service. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics – Website traffic analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense – Advertising service. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Netlify – Website hosting. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. Analytics data is retained by Google Analytics per their standard retention policies. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

ScrambleFix is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
