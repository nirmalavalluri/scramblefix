Why 2-Letter Words Matter in Scrabble

Two-letter words are essential in Scrabble and Words With Friends. They let you play parallel to existing words, create hooks for future plays, and use awkward letters like Q, X, Z, and J efficiently.

Most Useful 2-Letter Words

Some of the most valuable 2-letter words include: QI (the only Q word without U), ZA (short for pizza), XI, XU, AX, OX, EX, JO, and many more. Learning these words dramatically improves your game.
