What makes us different

Most word unscramblers use simple pattern matching against a static word list. ScrambleFix uses a fast word engine with AI-assisted logic to find every valid word — including rare 2-letter words, unusual combinations, and words that simpler tools miss.

Results are grouped by word length so you can instantly find the best plays — whether you need a quick 2-letter filler or a high-scoring 7-letter word.

Built for every word game

ScrambleFix works with Scrabble, Wordle, Words With Friends, crossword puzzles, word jumbles, and any other word game where you need to find words from a set of letters.

Free and always will be

ScrambleFix is completely free to use with no sign-up, no limits, and no paywalls. We keep the lights on through advertising — a small trade-off for a completely free tool.
